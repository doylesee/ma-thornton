Personalized, Meaningful Coaching

Too many people in the professional world don’t understand the power of mentoring. Simply put, coaching enables one to reach higher levels of performance than someone could achieve on their own.

The same philosophy that has helped countless athletes reach the pinnacle of performance applies perfectly to our professional and personal lives. Living up to your potential is so much easier with someone creating the structures you need to move forward.

What can M.A. Thornton’s coaching do for you?

Mat creates a safe, objective environment that allows his clients to see themselves far more clearly. Through a series of personalized, one-on-one appointments, participants are able to understand where they are and where they want to be.

One-on-One Sessions

Mat’s one-on-one sessions will build the foundation of forward movement by fostering intentional thought as well as action and behavior-based changes that will have a meaningful impact on someone’s approach to goal accomplishment.

The coaching style provides the crucial fusion of support and accountability. Mat helps to guide his clients down a new path through powerful mentoring. Yet, he also requires the people he coaches to put in the work necessary to meet their goals and realize their dreams.

Corporate Sessions

Inspire your workforce to take more initiative. Goal-oriented, career driven employees are the bedrock of success for any company.

Mat’s corporate sessions can help your workforce realize their potential. The benefits of workplace mentoring are well-documented. In fact, more than half of companies that embrace a culture of coaching report seeing an increase in revenue as a result.
